Freelance Creative Director and Multidisciplinary Designer with a passion for animation and over 12 years of diverse experience. As a key player in shaping creative strategies, I specialize in art direction, digital experiences, animation, brand design, and product design. I create impactful, high-end visuals that resonate with audiences and elevate brand stories. My portfolio includes collaborations with companies like Mojo, Makemepulse, WatsonDG, Mediamonks, and TBWA, and major brands across industries such as Netflix, Google, Nike, Awwwards, Adobe, Ubisoft, A24, and Disney.

I believe every brand and product has the potential to tell a powerful story that connects emotionally with people. Whether working with production studios, advertising agencies, or startups, I strive for design excellence, compelling storytelling, and playful experiences. Throughout my career, I’ve demonstrated strong leadership in building and managing design teams, fostering collaboration, efficiency, and creative excellence. Beyond design, I’m also an entrepreneur, design coach, and consultant, helping companies with both strategic and design expertise.
